# Design.md

## Brand Foundation

The brand is built around a single idea:

**Sharpening Ideas.**

The sharpener is not a stationery object.

It is a symbol of refinement.

Raw thoughts enter.

Clear ideas emerge.

Every visual decision should reinforce this concept.

---

# Brand Personality

The brand should feel:

* Creative
* Intelligent
* Curious
* Human
* Inventive
* Thoughtful
* Precise
* Optimistic

The brand should NOT feel:

* Corporate
* Enterprise
* Cold
* Futuristic-for-the-sake-of-it
* Generic AI startup
* Developer-only

Think:

Creative Engineer.

Not:

Corporate Software.

---

# Core Symbol

The sharpener glyph is the center of the visual identity.

It represents:

* Learning
* Refinement
* Improvement
* Creativity
* Thinking
* Knowledge
* Craftsmanship

The symbol should always feel:

* Solid
* Geometric
* Engineered
* Recognizable

Never:

* Cartoonish
* Cute
* Overly detailed
* Realistic

---

# Design Philosophy

The visual language combines two opposing forces:

## Precision

Represented by:

* Geometric logo
* Structured layouts
* Clear hierarchy
* Intentional spacing

## Creativity

Represented by:

* Excalifont
* Sketch-like feeling
* Human imperfections
* Warm colors

The tension between precision and creativity is the brand.

# Typography

## Primary Typeface

Excalifont-Regular.woff2

The entire brand uses Excalifont.

This is a strategic choice.

Most AI products use:

* Inter
* Geist
* SF Pro
* Helvetica

These communicate software.

Excalifont communicates ideas.

The brand should feel like a sketchbook for thinking.

---

## Typography Principles

Typography should feel:

* Human
* Expressive
* Approachable
* Creative

Never:

* Formal
* Corporate
* Mechanical

# Logo Style

## Construction

All symbols should feel carved from a single object.

Use:

* Geometric masses
* Polygonal forms
* Faceted surfaces
* Negative space

Avoid:

* Thin line icons
* Multiple floating pieces
* Decorative details

---

## Negative Space

Meaning should come from subtraction.

The sharpener uses:

* Blade slot
* Screw hole
* Internal cuts

Future icons should use similar techniques.

---

## Visual Weight

Icons should feel:

* Bold
* Strong
* Stable

Never fragile.

Never thin.

---

# Iconography

Icons should feel like tools.

Not UI symbols.

Examples:

Good:

* Sharpener
* Pencil
* Book
* Spark
* Compass
* Light source

Bad:

* Generic AI brain
* Robot head
* Circuit board
* Hexagon logo
* Infinity symbol

---

# Motion

Animations should feel:

* Intentional
* Smooth
* Helpful

Not playful.

Not bouncy.

Motion should suggest:

Refinement.

Not entertainment.

---

# Illustrations

Illustrations should feel:

* Hand-crafted
* Sketch-like
* Simple
* Geometric

Inspired by:

* Excalidraw
* Notebook sketches
* Whiteboard thinking

Avoid:

* 3D renders
* Stock illustrations
* Corporate vectors

# Brand Test

Every design decision should pass this test:

Does this help the brand feel like a place where ideas are sharpened?

If not, remove it.

The goal is not to look like an AI company.

The goal is to look like the best place for thinking.
